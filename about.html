About

My Story

Hi, I’m Jeremy, a user experience and interface designer living in Columbus, Ohio. As a designer I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with peers and a variety of stakeholders to dream up and implement some great experiences for those who need it most. Throughout my career, I've always cared deeply about solving problems for real people by using creative solutions. Working in the non-profit sector for 7 years I was uniquely equipped to understand and empathize with people’s needs by facilitating in-depth interviews, surveys, and workshops. As a self-taught graphic designer turned creative director I also utilized and developed my design, communication, and management skills while seeking to make a difference. As a UX and UI designer, I believe in creating simple, usable and engaging experiences for users that help them achieve the goals that matter to them. Truly getting to know users is foundational to my design process, as is broad collaboration with my team, efficient performance in my work, and continual learning through it all. When I'm not designing, you can find me hiking outside, playing fetch with my dog, or passionately competing in a wide variety of board games with my friends.
